The World's Best Cheese Soup

This cheese soup does it all. Crippling debt? Make some cheese soup. Divorce? Cheese soup is there to support you. Need help financing a new car? Well cheese soup probably won't help you there but have I ever told you about my time in 1936 where...

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 4 tablespoons margarine
  • 6 cups peeled and cubed butternut squash
  • 3 cups water
  • 4 cubes chicken bouillon
  • ½ teaspoon dried marjoram
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, saute onions in margarine until tender. Add squash, water, bouillon, marjoram, black pepper and cayenne pepper. Bring to boil; cook 20 minutes, or until squash is tender.
  2. Puree squash and cream cheese in a blender or food processor in batches until smooth. Return to saucepan, and heat through. Do not allow to boil.
